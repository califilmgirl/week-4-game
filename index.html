Crystal Collector Game!

Let's Play
Step 1: You'll get a random number at the start of the game.

Step 2: By clicking on on of the crystals below, you'll be given a random point value that will add up your total score as you continue to click.

Win by matching your total score to the random number. Lose if your totals above the random number.

You won't know the crystal value until you click on it.

Everytime you play, the game will change the values of each crystal. It's a suprise each game!
